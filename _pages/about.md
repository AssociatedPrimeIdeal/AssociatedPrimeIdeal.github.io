I am Yuyang Ren (任宇扬 in Chinese), a PhD student in Computer Science at ShanghaiTech University in Shanghai, China, where I am advised by Prof. [Peng Hu](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=5LVIHHoAAAAJ&hl=en).

Before joining ShanghaiTech, I received my bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Shanghai University in 2022.

My research focuses on 4D flow MRI, particularly sequence design, reconstruction, and post-processing.
