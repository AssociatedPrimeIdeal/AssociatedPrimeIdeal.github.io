I am Yuyang Ren (任宇扬), a PhD student in the School of Biomedical Engineering at ShanghaiTech University, Shanghai, China.

I am advised by [Peng Hu](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=5LVIHHoAAAAJ&hl=en).

My research focuses on 4D flow MRI, including sequence design, reconstruction, and post-processing.

For a current publication list, please see the [Publications](/publications/) page or my [Google Scholar profile](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=5L1wFjUAAAAJ&hl=en). Code and research utilities are available on [GitHub](https://github.com/AssociatedPrimeIdeal).
